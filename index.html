CHEF MATEO RIVAS HEAD CHEF · OWNER

Brasa is the vision of Chef Mateo, shaped by his Oaxacan roots and years in some of the world's most respected fire kitchens.

Each plate at Brasa is a letter home. A slow-cooked barbacoa lamb, a prime ribeye kissed by mesquite, lobster bathed in chile de árbol butter — every bite travels between memory and reinvention.

Our kitchen is open flame only. No shortcuts, no hiding. Just embers, iron, and the patience of hands that know the fire.

FAQS

Do I need to book a table in advance?

We recommend reserving at least 2 weeks ahead, especially for weekends and the chef's counter.

Where do your ingredients come from?

We source prime cuts from small ranches in Sonora, lobster from Baja California, and produce from local Mexican farms.

Do you cater for dietary needs?

Yes. Vegetarian, gluten-free and allergy-aware menus are available with 24-hour notice.

Can I host a private event at Brasa?

Absolutely. The private Ember Room seats up to 18 guests for tasting menus and celebrations.

Is there parking nearby?

Valet service is available from 6pm. Secure parking is located one block south.

What are your opening hours?

Tuesday to Sunday, 6pm — 11pm. Closed Mondays.
